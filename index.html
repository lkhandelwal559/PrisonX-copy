About

What you think about us ?

At the E-Portal for Under Trial Prisoners, we believe in the transformative power of information, education, and support. Our initiative is driven by a vision to empower individuals in the legal system, specifically those navigating the challenges of being undertrial prisoners. Our Mission: We are on a mission to bridge the gap between justice and accessibility. Through a multifaceted approach, we strive to provide undertrial prisoners with the tools they need not only to understand and navigate their legal journey but also to embark on a path of personal growth, rehabilitation, and reintegration into society.
